Puffalot Rebrand

Puffalot is a local smoke shop in Ottawa, Ontario. The goal of this rebrand was to give Puffalot an appearance that would display their level of professionalism to the rest of the industry.

The scope of this project involved a full rebrand. The deliverables included a new logo, a branding guide, a full set of stationary, and a website concept.
