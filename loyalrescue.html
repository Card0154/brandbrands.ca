Loyal Rescue Website Overhaul

Loyal Rescue is a foster-based rescue located in Peterborough, Ontario. This project involved giving their website a face lift in order to help their clients interact with their services.

The process involved sketching, mockups, and coding the majority of the pages. The end result is an accessible website without all the visual noise that regularly accompanies animal adoption pages.
